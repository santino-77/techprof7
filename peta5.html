TOURIST DESTINATION

HOME PLACES FOOD

LUZON


Sisig
A sizzling dish made from chopped pork, onions, and chili, seasoned with calamansi and spices. It is known for its savory, tangy, and slightly spicy flavor.
Bulalo
A hearty beef soup made by simmering beef shanks and bone marrow for hours with vegetables like cabbage, corn, and potatoes. It is famous for its rich, comforting broth.
Pinakbet
A vegetable stew made with squash, eggplant, bitter melon, okra, and string beans, flavored with shrimp paste. It has a savory taste with a hint of bitterness.

VISAYAS


Chicken Inasal
Grilled chicken marinated in vinegar, calamansi, garlic, lemongrass, and annatto oil. It is juicy, smoky, and often served with rice and chicken oil.
La Paz Batchoy
A flavorful noodle soup made with pork, liver, crushed chicharon, and egg noodles in a rich pork broth. It is a comforting and hearty meal.
Kansi
A sour beef soup made with beef shanks, jackfruit, and batwan fruit. It combines the richness of bulalo with a distinctly sour flavor.

MINDANAO


Pastil
Steamed rice topped with shredded chicken, beef, or fish, wrapped in banana leaves. It is a simple, affordable, and filling meal.
Satti
Grilled beef or chicken skewers served with a spicy, flavorful sauce and rice. It is a popular breakfast dish with a rich and slightly sweet taste.
Piyanggang Manok (tawi-tawi)
Chicken cooked in a sauce made from burnt coconut meat, coconut milk, and local spices. It has a smoky, creamy, and savory flavor.
