LUZON


Taal Volcano Lake
Taal Lake is located in the province of Batangas, on the island of Luzon, Philippines, approximately 50 kilometers (31 miles) south of Manila. Moreover, Taal Volcano Lake (Taal Lake) is a scenic freshwater caldera lake that surrounds Taal Volcano, one of the most active volcanoes in the Philippines.
Banaue Rice Terraces
The Banaue Rice Terraces are located in the municipality of Banaue, in the Ifugao Province of northern Luzon. They are approximately 350 kilometers (217 miles) north of Manila. The Banaue Rice Terraces are a remarkable network of ancient rice terraces carved into the mountains of northern Luzon by the Ifugao people over 2,000 years ago.
Manila Ocean Park
Manila Ocean Park is located behind the Quirino Grandstand in Rizal Park (Luneta), Ermita, Manila, Metro Manila, Philippines. The Manila Ocean Park is the Philippines' first world-class marine theme park and oceanarium, offering visitors an educational and interactive experience with marine life.
Fort Santiago
Fort Santiago is located within the historic walled city of Intramuros, in Manila. It stands at the mouth of the Pasig River, near Manila Bay. The Fort Santiago is a historic citadel and one of the most important landmarks in the Philippines. Built by the Spanish in 1571 under Miguel López de Legazpi.
Rizal Park
Rizal Park is located in Ermita, Manila, along Roxas Boulevard, beside Manila Bay. Rizal Park, also known as Luneta Park, is one of the most famous historical parks in the Philippines. It is a large urban park dedicated to Dr. José Rizal, the country's national hero.

VISAYAS


Pescador Island
Pescador Island is located in the Tañon Strait, approximately 3 kilometers off the coast of Moalboal in the province of Cebu, Philippines. It is known for its crystal-clear waters, colorful coral reefs, and abundant sea life, including schools of sardines, sea turtles, and various tropical fish
Manjuyod White Sandbar
The sandbar is located in the municipality of Manjuyod, in the province of Negros Oriental, Philippines. It lies in the Tañon Strait, off the coast of Manjuyod and near the city of Bais. Manjuyod White Sandbar is a stunning stretch of fine white sand that emerges during low tide, often earning the nickname “the Maldives of the Philippines”.
Crystal Cove Island
Crystal Cove Island is located off the coast of Boracay Island in the municipality of Malay, Aklan, Philippines. Situated in the Sibuyan Sea. Crystal Cove Island is a privately owned resort island known for its beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and unique natural attractions.
Can Umanted Falls
Can-Umantad Falls is located in Barangay Cadapdapan, in the municipality of Candijay, Bohol, Philippines. It is situated in the eastern part of Bohol, about 100 kilometers from Tagbilaran City. Can-Umantad Falls is the tallest waterfall in the province of Bohol and one of the most impressive natural attractions in the region.
Mount Luho
Mount Luho is located in Barangay Yapak on Boracay Island, within the municipality of Malay, Aklan, Philippines. The area around Mount Luho is also home to diverse plants and wildlife, making it an ideal spot for sightseeing and nature lovers.

MINDANAO


Ardent Hot Spring
Ardent Hot Spring is located in the municipality of Mambajao, on Camiguin Island, in the province of Camiguin, Philippines. It lies at the foot of Mount Hibok-Hibok, surrounded by lush tropical vegetation, the hot spring offers visitors a relaxing and refreshing experience. 		img src="cannibud-mindanao.JPG" width="220px" height="220px">
Canibud Beach
Canibad Beach is located in Barangay Adecor, within the Island Garden City of Samal (IGaCoS), in the province of Davao del Norte, Philippines. Canibad Beach is a beautiful and relatively secluded beach known for its white sand, clear blue waters, and peaceful atmosphere.
Lake Holon
Lake Holon is located in the municipality of Tboli, in the province of South Cotabato, Philippines. It lies within the crater of Mount Parker, approximately 30 kilometers southwest of Koronadal City. Formed within the crater of Mount Melibengoy, also known as Mount Parker, the lake is considered one of the cleanest inland bodies of water in the Philippines.
Cagwait White Beach
Cagwait White Beach is located in the municipality of Cagwait, in the province of Surigao del Sur, Philippines. The beach features a crescent-shaped shoreline lined with coconut trees, making it an ideal spot for swimming, sunbathing, picnics, and beach camping.
Sohoton Lagoon
Sohoton Lagoon is located within the Sohoton Cove National Park on Bucas Grande Island, in the municipality of Socorro, Surigao del Norte, Philippines. It is best known for its enchanting scenery and unique ecosystem, which includes hidden coves, rock formations, and the nearby Jellyfish Sanctuary, where visitors can swim with non-stinging jellyfish during certain seasons.
